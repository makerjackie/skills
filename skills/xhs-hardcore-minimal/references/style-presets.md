# Style Presets

## hardcore-minimal (default)
- Background: `#F7F7F5`
- Text: `#101010`
- Accent: `#111111`
- Mood: calm, serious, dense
- Use: practical tutorials, frameworks, methods

## mono-grid
- Background: `#FFFFFF`
- Text: `#0A0A0A`
- Accent: `#D9D9D9`
- Mood: editorial, structured
- Use: checklists, matrices, comparisons

## neo-brutal
- Background: `#FFF455`
- Text: `#111111`
- Accent: `#FF3B30`
- Mood: bold, loud, high contrast
- Use: breakthrough claims, strong hooks

## dark-tech
- Background: `#0B1020`
- Text: `#EAF2FF`
- Accent: `#4DF0B8`
- Mood: technical, futuristic
- Use: AI, automation, tools, analytics
